M O R E







Baking macarons is fun but extremely challenging for first timers. There are a lot of tedious steps that you need to take, and a lot can go wrong if you’re not careful. To alleviate some of the stress, I decided to create an instructional video that breaks down the tools and steps to bake vanilla macarons. The video begins with differentiating macarons from macaroons then proceeds to listing the essential ingredients and baking tools. I carefully describe and showcase how each step should look to eliminate potential baking mistakes. Once the viewers learn the baking steps, they will examine common mistakes that can happen with macarons. After learning about macaron troubleshoots, the viewers will test their baking knowledge through scenario-based learning activities.

By creating an instructional video, learners have the opportunity to visualize the objectives and steps to be set up for success. Additionally, audiovisual learning provides the user the chance to digest the information at a pace that is comfortable for them. The ability to adjust the volume, playback speed, stop/start time, and add subtitles makes the learning accessible to a larger target audience as well.
